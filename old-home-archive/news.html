Why I haven't added particles in FCC DE

Hey guys, Jaydar here. This is a short article; I know, but I feel like it's worth mentioning, as there haven't been any major announcements about FCC DE recently. You may have been thinking, "Particles! Add particles, Jaydar!"
The truth is, I tried that months ago, but it just didn't work out. I had an old video of exactly what would happen, but that file has since been wiped of the face of the planet. Essentially, what happened was:

  1. I clicked on the cookie
  2. The game displayed a huge vertical line of teeny, tiny cookies
  3. The game froze as the Oh Noes Guy was thinking
  4. The game then crashed completely as the Oh Noes Guy puked out an error message that went something like "%%ErrorMessage%%Perhaps you made a mistake in your code?"

Yeah, I still have zero idea what that "mistake in [my] code" could be, or what's up with the "%%ErrorMessage%%" part. Maybe it's possible to do it, but I have no idea how, if it is possible.
