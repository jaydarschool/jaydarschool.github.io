funky-loookin stuff


funky-lookin stuff (yes, that's it's REAL NAME,) is a one-off project, but it's kind of hard to really call it a "game."
It's more of me just testing the waters when it comes to interactive JS programs.
The program draws circles that are almost 100% based on your mouse's location (ie color, size, location, ect.)
You can make some cool-looking... uh... things with this.
Not to mention that the code is super simple, so funky-lookin stuff can run off of most anything.


funky-lookin stuff 2: the funkying


Funky-lookin stuff 2 is a sequel to funky-lookin stuff that is more complicated, but not by much.
It builds off of the idea in its predacessor with it still using lots of parameters from your mouse, but now it has 4 circles that all should have different colors, based on your mouse.
It uses a special timer that changes the background color around every 15 seconds.


funky-lookin stuff 3: the threequel


This is a threequel to funky-lookin stuff, but this time, it uses a lot more random factors to judge aspects about the circles.
There's still the 15-second background change from funky-lookin stuff 2, though.
